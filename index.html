About Me

I have been living in Seattle for around 5 years now. Most recently I worked at GeneralUI as an SDET, where I did manual testing and some automation for the many projects that the company developed. Spanning technologies from the basic node javascript web applications, iOS, Android, and Rails.

I have also worked with several test automation frameworks, Cucumber via Capybara in Ruby, Mocha/Chai, and a bit of RSPEC. I have worked with ghekin, written test cases, test plans, and functional outlines. I have helped maintain MySQL and PostgreSQL databases. I also have worked closely with Project Managers/Owners and UX designers, to establish and maintain UX as a project changes over time.

I know many popular business practices like agile developement, scrum, test driven development, behavior driven development and I am familiar with the concepts behind XP programming. Currently I am transitioning from a QA role to a Developer role by taking classes at Codefellows to help round out my skills.
